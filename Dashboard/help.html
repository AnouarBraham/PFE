Help

This is the help page. You can find assistance and support here.

Dashboard

The dashboard displays various metrics and statistics about KPIs (Key Performance Indicators). You can find information about sales, stores, products and more.

How do I navigate the dashboard?

To navigate the dashboard, use the sidebar on the left side of the screen. Click on the different sections to view various data and information.

User Profile

If you need help with your user profile, you can find assistance here.

How can I change my profile picture?

You can change your profile picture from the main menu on the first page. Click on your profile icon, then select a picture to upload as a new image.

How can I change my profile informations?

You can change your profile's firstname, lastname, email and password from the profile page on the sidebar on the left.

Dark Mode

Dark mode provides a different color scheme for the website, reducing eye strain in low-light environments.

How do I activate dark mode?

To activate dark mode, click on the moon icon located next to the notificaton icon on the top of the page.
